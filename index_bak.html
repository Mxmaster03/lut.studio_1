About
"Every frame is a quiet negotiation between light and what it chooses to reveal."

Charlton Sia is a Singapore-based photographer with a focus on portrait, editorial, and fashion photography. Working under the studio name C_Studio03, his approach is rooted in stillness — finding the unguarded moment within the deliberate.

With a background in fine art and over five years of professional practice, Charlton has worked with independent brands, editorial publications, and private clients across Singapore and Southeast Asia.
